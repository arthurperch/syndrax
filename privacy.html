Privacy Policy

How Syndrax Sync handles your data. Last updated: June 2026.

Syndrax Sync (“the extension”) helps sellers automate eCommerce tasks on supported marketplaces such as eBay and Amazon.

Data We Handle

  • Settings and listing data you create are stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage) on your own device.
  • The extension reads and interacts with content on marketplace pages you visit in order to perform its functions (for example, creating listings or reading orders). This processing happens locally in your browser.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for any purpose unrelated to the extension’s single purpose (eCommerce automation).
  • We do not use your data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
